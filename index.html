Toom Sauce Original

Description

Our Toom Sauce is an authentic Lebanese, vegan, gluten free dip/sauce made only with natural ingredients and no fillers.

The OG Toom Sauce is perfectly prepared with only four simple ingredients, the main one being fresh garlic. It is creamy and packs a punch with a wonderful garlic aroma.

Toom Sauce is a dip, spread, marinate, salad dressing and cooking base, therefor it can be added to your favorite recipe in any way you like.

Ingredients:

  • Non-GMO Canola Oil
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Himalayan pink salt
